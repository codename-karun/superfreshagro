Know About Us




    • At Super Fresh Agro, we are committed to delivering excellence to our valued customers. From the very beginning, our core philosophy has centered on a simple yet powerful idea: providing nothing less than nature’s best to our clients.

    Founded in 2018, Super Fresh Agro is an extension of a family with a long-standing tradition in the fruit business. With extensive knowledge and a great reputation in fresh and processed fruits. Super Fresh is renowned for its production and supply of fruit pulps, purees, pastes, and concentrates. We cater to both international and domestic markets with products made from mango, guava, pineapple, papaya, banana, tomato, lychee, and various other fruits.

Processing fruit pulp is central to our company’s operations. We package a variety of fruit pulps, purees, and concentrates, adapting to each fruit's seasonal availability. Based in South India, our primary products include mango pulp and mango concentrate, followed by guava pulp, papaya concentrate, pineapple puree, and tomato paste. The tropical climate of our region ensures that these fruits are abundant and deliver exceptional natural taste, flavor, aroma, and freshness.

At Super Fresh Agro, our dedicated team of passionate professionals is committed to exceeding client expectations. We adhere to stringent measures at every stage—from sourcing the finest fruits and processing fruit pulp to packaging in various sizes and delivering with the quickest turnaround time. Our goal is to consistently meet and surpass customer expectations.

Our relentless pursuit of quality has enabled us to achieve and maintain international standards, earning us recognition as a premier processor and exporter of tropical fruit pulps and concentrates from India. We take immense pride in our partnerships with esteemed brands in the Middle East, Europe and Africa.

Visions and Missions

    • Our vision is to become a leading supplier of fruit pulp, committed to upholding the highest standards of quality and sustainability. We collaborate closely with local farmers, orchard owners, and fruit societies to source the finest fruits and develop innovative techniques to enhance yield and expand production areas. By engaging with our customers and coordinating with our processors, we ensure that our products are packaged to meet the highest hygiene and global standards. Our dedicated logistics team guarantees timely deliveries and exceptional service to all our customers.

    These collaborations reflect our commitment to fostering mutually beneficial relationships built on trust, respect, and a shared vision for excellence.
